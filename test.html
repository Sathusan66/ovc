2020

Seeds Ball

We proudly present our innovative project: Seed Balls Nature's Gift, Reimagined Through Technology. This eco-friendly initiative is designed to promote sustainable reforestation using organic methods. By combining traditional wisdom with modern scientific thinking, our students create seed balls using native seeds and natural compost, allowing vegetation to grow even in harsh and barren lands.

This project reflects our commitment to environmental protection, sustainability, and the use of technology to make a positive impact on the world. Through this initiative, we aim to inspire young minds to think green and act responsibly because even a small seed can spark a big change.
