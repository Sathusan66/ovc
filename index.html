The Foundation of Our Technology Unit

The Technology Unit of T/Orr’s Hill Vivekananda College stands as a symbol of progress, innovation, and dedication to education. Laid on a strong foundation, our unit was officially inaugurated through the vision and leadership of R. Sampanthan MP and K. Thurairetnasingham MP, who laid the foundation stone for this initiative. From the very beginning, our mission has been to bridge technology with real-world solutions, empowering students with knowledge and practical skills. One of our most innovative projects, the Seed Ball Initiative, reflects our commitment to sustainability and eco-friendly technology. Through this project, we integrate science and environmental responsibility, creating a greener tomorrow with technology driven solutions. Our journey continues with unwavering passion, inspiring future generations to explore, innovate, and transform ideas into reality.

Housewarming Ceremony of Our Technology Unit

The opening of the Technology Unit at T/Orr’s Hill Vivekananda College was marked by a heartfelt and traditional housewarming ceremony, deeply rooted in Tamil cultural values. The event symbolized not just the beginning of an academic journey, but also the spiritual and cultural foundation of our unit. With the aroma of incense, the lighting of the Kuthu Vilakku (traditional oil lamp), and the recitation of blessings and prayers, the ceremony embraced the rich heritage of our community. Traditional music, offerings, and rituals were performed with great devotion, seeking prosperity, wisdom, and positive energy for the future of our students and staff. This celebration was a beautiful reminder that while we grow in technology and innovation, we remain grounded in our cultural roots and respect for tradition.

Official Opening A Proud Milestone in Our Journey

The Technology Unit of T/Orr’s Hill Vivekananda College was officially declared open by Mr. K. Thurairetnasingham MP, marking a significant milestone in the history of our institution. This proud moment took place during the dedicated leadership of our honorable principal, Mr. K. Ravithas Sir, whose unwavering commitment to academic excellence and student empowerment played a vital role in making this dream a reality. The opening ceremony was not just an event, but a celebration of vision, hard work, and a strong belief in the power of education and technology. With the blessings of our leaders and the support of our community, our Technology Unit was born a space where creativity meets innovation, and young minds are nurtured to become futureready technologists.
