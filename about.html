About Our Technology Stream

Welcome to the Technology Stream of Vivekananda College, Trincomalee. Our stream was established with the vision of providing quality technological education to students in the Eastern Province of Sri Lanka.

The foundation stone for our Technology Unit was proudly laid on 02.02.2017 by R. Sampanthan MP and K. Thurairetnasingham MP, symbolizing the beginning of a new era in technology education at T/Orr’s Hill Vivekananda College. Officially opened on 01.03.2019 by Mr. K. Thurairetnasingham MP during the leadership of our honorable principal Mr. K. Ravithas, our unit started with only basic lab equipment but filled with big dreams. Despite limited resources, our passion, dedication, and the unwavering support of our teachers led us to remarkable achievements. From small facilities, we grew steadily, producing impactful, innovative projects and reaching significant milestones, including an inspiring growth in university entrances. Our journey is proof that greatness begins with a single step, and determination can turn small beginnings into powerful success stories.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for higher education and careers in various technology fields.

Our Technology Units

The Technology Stream at Vivekananda College offers comprehensive education through the following specialized units:

Engineering Technology

Focuses on mechanical and civil engineering principles with practical applications.

  • Mechanical Systems
  • Structural Design
  • Materials Technology
  • Engineering Drawing

Biosystems Technology

Combines biological sciences with technology for agricultural and environmental applications.

  • Agricultural Technology
  • Food Processing
  • Environmental Systems
  • Biotechnology

Science for Technology

Provides the scientific foundation for all technology disciplines.

  • Applied Physics
  • Applied Chemistry
  • Applied Mathematics
  • Technical English

Our Achievements

Since its inception, our Technology Stream has achieved remarkable success:

  • Consistently high pass rates in G.C.E. Advanced Level Examinations
  • Numerous district and national awards in technology competitions
  • Successful university placements for our graduates
  • Industry partnerships for student internships and training
  • Recognition as a center of excellence for technology education in the Eastern Province
